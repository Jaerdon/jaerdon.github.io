Electric Boat Project




What is it?

This is a boat I designed with my friend Ethan for our physics class, then printed, built, and tested.

The Process

When we first designed this boat, we sat down and discussed designs and materials for the project. We decided upon 3D printing the body, which I would model and fabricate, and the electronics Ethan got and did the calculations for.


I then printed and tested the boat for the first time. Unfortunately, when trying to add any weight inside the boat, it was just slightly too heavy.

Luckily, I was able to model and print attachments that would act similarly to empty barrels and help the boat float just enough.


The electronics were then installed and the boat was ready for actual testing.
